Conway's Game Of Life

by Leander Wissert

For reference: Game Of Life Wiki

Shortcuts:

Style:
- Press "b","n","m" for different colormodes.
- Press "o" and "p" to change the shape of the cells.
General:
- Press "a" to Start.
- Press "s" to Stop.
- Press "d" to see the next Generation.
---
- Press "c" to clear the canvas.
- Press "r" to randomly place cells on the canvas.
---
- Press "t" for torodial Field (edges are connected)
- Press "f" for finite Field (edges are cut off)

Shortcuts:

Style:
- Press "f" for black background and white cells.
- Press "g" for white background and black cells.
- Press "h" for the best colormode!
- Press "o" and "p" to change the shape of the cells.
General:
- Press"j" to Start.
- Press "k" to Stop.
- Press "l" to see the next Generation.
- Press "c" to clear the canvas.
- Press "r" to randomly place cells on the canvas.
Resolution:
- Press "a" to increase the resolution.
- Press "s" to decrease the resolution.
-Press "d" to reset the resolution.
Size of the Canvas:
- Press "q" to increase the size of the canvas.
- Press "w" to decrease the size of the canvas.
- Press "e" to reset the size of the canvas.
